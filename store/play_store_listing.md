### Full Description
*(max 4000 characters)*
```
తెలుగు వర్ణమాల (Telugu Varnamala) is a fun and colourful app that helps
kids learn all 52 letters of the Telugu alphabet — vowels (అచ్చులు) and
consonants (హల్లులు).

✨ FEATURES

• Browse all 52 Telugu letters in a colourful grid
• Tap any letter to see its illustration, example word, and meaning
• Swipe left or right to move through letters one by one
• Audio pronunciation plays automatically for every letter
• Track your progress — learned letters are highlighted with a star ⭐
• Quiz mode: hear a sound and pick the matching letter
• Confetti celebration for correct quiz answers 🎉

📚 WHAT YOUR CHILD WILL LEARN

• All 16 vowels (అచ్చులు): అ ఆ ఇ ఈ ఉ ఊ ఋ ఎ ఏ ఐ ఒ ఓ ఔ అం అః ళ
• All 36 consonants (హల్లులు): క ఖ గ ఘ చ ఛ జ ఝ ట ఠ డ ఢ త థ ద ధ న ప ఫ బ భ మ య ర ల వ శ ష స హ and more
• Each letter paired with a familiar Telugu word and its English meaning
• Clear audio pronunciation for every letter

🎯 DESIGNED FOR KIDS

• Large, bold Telugu characters easy to read on any screen
• Bright colours and simple illustrations
• No ads, no in-app purchases

Perfect for kids aged 3–8, parents, and anyone learning Telugu as a
second language.

తెలుగు నేర్చుకోండి! Learn Telugu today!
```
